# Viral Marketing Strategy Specification (v1.0 - Growth Engine Focus)

## 1. Marketing Core Philosophy

**"Every share is a salesperson"** - We don't just enable sharing, we engineer contagious moments that make users our most effective marketers. Every touchpoint is optimized for **measurable viral coefficient > 1.0**.

## 3. Launch Campaign Phases

### **Phase 1: Seeding (Week 1-2)**
**Objective**: 1,000 initial users with 40% viral growth

#### **Micro-Influencer Program**
- **Target**: 50 creators (1k-50k followers)
- **Compensation**: Early access + exclusive Persona frame
- **Content**: "I let AI analyze my personality based on my friends' opinions..."

#### **Campus Ambassador Network**
- **Universities**: Top 10 Korean universities
- **Ambassadors**: 5 students per campus
- **Tactics**: 
  - KakaoTalk open chat rooms
  - Instagram story takeovers
  - Offline QR code stickers

#### **Beta Tester Rewards**
- **Mechanic**: First 500 users get "Founder" badge
- **Incentive**: Permanent profile customization
- **Goal**: Create FOMO for public launch

### **Phase 2: Viral Acceleration (Week 3-4)**
**Objective**: 10,000 users with K-factor > 1.2

#### **"First Share" Campaign**
- **Trigger**: User's first Persona reveal
- **Reward**: Unlock premium archetype
- **Message**: "Share to unlock rare Persona types"

#### **"Friend Challenge" Series**
- **Concept**: "Tag 3 friends who need this"
- **Mechanic**: Tagged friends get priority queue
- **Viral Boost**: Creates social pressure loop

#### **Platform-Specific Content**
- **Instagram**: AR filter "What Persona are you?"
- **TikTok**: Trending audio + Persona reveal
- **Twitter**: Daily "Persona of the day" thread

### **Phase 3: Retention Virality (Week 5-8)**
**Objective**: 50,000 users with 30% weekly active sharing

#### **Weekly Persona Updates**
- **Trigger**: New personality insights
- **Format**: "This week, I learned I'm actually..."
- **KPI**: 25% of users share weekly updates

#### **"Persona Battle" Events**
- **Concept**: Compare Personas with friends
- **Mechanic**: Share battle results
- **Viral Element**: Winner gets featured on explore page

## 7. Risk Mitigation

### **Viral Fatigue Prevention**
- **Content refresh**: New templates every 2 weeks
- **Feature drops**: Seasonal Persona themes
- **Gamification**: Achievement system for sharers

### **Platform Policy Changes**
- **Backup channels**: Email, Discord communities
- **Owned media**: Push notifications, in-app sharing
- **Cross-platform**: Never rely on single channel >30%

### **Negative Viral Response**
- **Sentiment monitoring**: Real-time social listening
- **Crisis response**: 2-hour response team
- **Positive amplification**: Highlight wholesome shares
